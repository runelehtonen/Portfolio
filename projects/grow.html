GROW

Project description (2. Semester - Group project)

For our first group project of 2nd semester, we worked closely with a part of EAAA called GROW. GROW is a small community for current and former students, with a common interest in entrepreneurship. We developed a webdocumentary and SoMe-strategy, in which we showcased what GROW has to offer.
